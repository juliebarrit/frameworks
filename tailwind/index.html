My expertise lies in seamlessly combining front-end and back-end operations to create a smooth and cohesive website experience. Interactivity, sustainability, and accessibility are at the core of my work, ensuring an engaging user experience that keeps visitors interested and exploring.

I prioritize simplicity and dynamic design while maintaining a structure that is easy to update, scalable, and built for long-term growth.

I'm Julie, a Danish full-stack developer currently diving into web development. I’m a bit of a homebody - nothing beats unwinding with video games or getting lost in nature, especially in places filled with trees.

I love expressing my creativity through web design and occasionally through painting (even if my skills still need some work… for now). When I’m not coding or creating, I spend as much time as I can with friends and family.
